🚀 Project Name

👉 ResilientAI: Autonomous Supply Intelligence for MSMEs

🧠 1. PROBLEM STATEMENT (FINAL)
Writing

In an increasingly volatile global environment, supply chain disruptions caused by geopolitical conflicts, energy crises, and price fluctuations directly impact small and medium businesses (MSMEs).

For instance, disruptions in critical trade routes such as the Strait of Hormuz can lead to sudden spikes in LPG and fuel prices, which in turn affect transportation costs, product pricing, and availability for local businesses like kirana stores.

However, these businesses lack real-time intelligence and decision-support systems to anticipate such disruptions and respond effectively. Existing tools are reactive and data-heavy, requiring manual interpretation and delayed decision-making.

There is a critical need for an intelligent system that can detect global risks, predict their local business impact, and provide actionable recommendations in real time, enabling MSMEs to operate resiliently in uncertain conditions.
